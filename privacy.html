Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2, 2026

1. Introduction

PDFUnlock is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, your rights regarding your data, and how to contact us with privacy concerns.

By using PDFUnlock, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. What Information We Collect

Your PDF Documents

  • Your PDF files are not uploaded to our servers
  • All processing occurs entirely on your local device
  • You retain complete control over your files at all times

Purchase Information

  • In-app purchase history is managed by Apple, not stored by PDFUnlock
  • Payment details are never collected by us — handled exclusively by Apple's App Store

Support Contact

  • If you contact us with support requests, we collect only the information you provide in that message
  • This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry and is not stored permanently

What We Do Not Collect

  • ✓ None  Crash reports or error logs
  • ✓ None  Usage analytics or statistics
  • ✓ None  In-app behavior tracking
  • ✓ None  Device identifiers, IP addresses, or location data

3. Local Storage & iCloud Sync

Keychain

  • Your free-unlock counter is encrypted and stored in your device's Keychain
  • Protected by iOS/macOS security and survives app reinstallation on the same device

iCloud Key-Value Store

  • If you are signed into iCloud, your free-unlock counter syncs across your devices
  • Only the counter is synced — your PDF documents are never uploaded to iCloud

How to Disable iCloud Sync

Go to Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud and toggle off PDFUnlock. The app will fall back to Keychain-only (device-specific) storage.

4. How We Use Your Information

Information Purpose
Support messages Respond to your inquiries only
Free-Unlock Counter Track free-tier usage on your device

We do not use your information for advertising, analytics, or any purpose beyond what is listed above.

5. Who We Share Your Information With

  • Apple: Your purchase history and payment information (governed by Apple's Privacy Policy)
  • iCloud Services: Your free-unlock counter, if you enable iCloud sync
  • No other third parties: We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any other parties

We may disclose information if legally required by law enforcement or court order.

6. Data Retention

Data Type Retention Period

  • Restrict or object to processing

  • File a complaint with your local data protection authority

CCPA (California Users)

Exercising Your Rights

To exercise your privacy rights, please contact us through the App Store support page for PDFUnlock. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Data Security

  • Local Processing: All PDF operations occur on your device, not on remote servers
  • Encryption: Data sent to Apple (iCloud, App Store) is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Keychain Encryption: Your counter is encrypted in your device's Keychain
  • No Central Database: We do not maintain centralized servers storing user data

While we implement reasonable security measures, no system is 100% secure.

9. Children's Privacy

PDFUnlock is not directed at children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover we have collected such data, we will delete it immediately. For EU users under 16, parental consent is required.

The App uses Apple's services (App Store, iCloud, Keychain, StoreKit 2). Apple's Privacy Policy governs data processed through these services. Visit apple.com/privacy for details.
